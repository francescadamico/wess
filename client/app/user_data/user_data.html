Our data are now available!

Contact us and we will be happy to provide you directly with the data you need.

  • 5 stations at 3 different locations: Entringen, Poltringen and Tailfingen.
  • 230 instruments, with 620 data channels (!).
  • Atmospheric measurements: temperature, humidity, wind speed and wind direction measured at two different levels.
  • Surface measurements: rain, soil heat flux, and sunlight.
  • Sub-surface measurements: temperature and moisture, at 5 levels to a maximum depth of 1.5 m.
